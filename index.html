What I Do

My Vision

"The organization is my customer. 'Great' is when programs, technology, and models are actually leveraged by teams — not because they're mandated, but because they're genuinely useful."

The best RevOps function is one you barely notice — because everything works. Forecasts are accurate. Territories are fair. The data is trusted. And every team has the tools and visibility they need to do their best work.

I let the field serve as our feedback mechanism. When we build against real friction points — the ones our reps and CSMs and marketers actually feel — we spend our time on initiatives that move the needle. That's the north star I work toward every day.
